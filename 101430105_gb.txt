1. George Brown College has 3 campuses downtown Toronto
    - Casa Loma campus
    - St. James campus
    - Waterfront campus

2. George Brown College was founded in 1967
3. George Brown College was named after George Brown, one of the Fathers of Confederation and founder of The Globe newspaper.
