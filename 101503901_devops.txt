COMP3104 - DevOps

Course Overview
This course teaches the procedures, protocols, and best practices that a DevOps engineer brings to a workplave environment to increase productivity.
Students will analyze and evaluate various developer operation implementations and create solutions to complex business problems by using an industry-standard toolkit.
